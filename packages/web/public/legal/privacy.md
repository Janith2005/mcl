# Privacy Policy

**Influence Pirates (MCL) -- Privacy Policy**

*Last updated: March 2026*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Influence Pirates ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Account information (name, email address)
- Workspace configuration and preferences
- Content you create or upload (brain data, scripts, analytics)
- Feedback and support communications

### Information Collected Automatically
- Usage data (features used, pages visited)
- Device information (browser type, operating system)
- Log data (IP address, access times)

### Third-Party Data
- Social media analytics data (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) that you connect to the Service
- Publicly available competitor data collected through our research tools

## 3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the Service
- To personalize your content coaching experience
- To process AI-powered content generation
- To analyze usage patterns and improve the Service
- To communicate with you about your account and updates

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
- Service providers (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
- AI model providers (for content generation -- your data is not used for model training)
- Law enforcement when required by law

## 5. Data Retention

- Active account data is retained for the duration of your account.
- Deleted workspace data is soft-deleted and permanently removed after 30 days.
- Analytics and usage logs are retained for 12 months.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Port your data to another service
- Object to processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@influencepirates.com.

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest
- Row-level security policies on all database tables
- Encrypted credential storage via Supabase Vault
- Regular security audits

## 8. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Analytics cookies (PostHog) are used with your consent.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes to this Privacy Policy via email or in-app notification.
