# Influence Pirates: Dual Design System Strategy

To provide both a high-energy creative environment and a focused, technical workspace, we are utilizing two distinct design systems.

---

## 1. Holo Prism (Light Mode)
**Creative North Star:** "The Luminous Lab"
**Core Aesthetic:** Vibrant, glassmorphic, and high-energy.

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Space Grotesk`
- **Characteristics:** High contrast, geometric, and modern. It provides a distinct personality that feels both experimental and professional.

### Color Palette
- **Primary Accents:** Fuchsia (#A666AA) to Pink gradients.
- **Backgrounds:** Soft off-whites with subtle glassmorphic overlays (blur + transparency).
- **Surface:** White with soft, colored shadows.

### UI Components
- **Roundness:** `ROUND_FULL` (Pill-shaped buttons and soft-radius cards).
- **Elevation:** Layered glass effects and subtle colored glows.

---

## 2. Obsidian Plunder (Dark Mode)
**Creative North Star:** "The Sovereign Navigator"
**Core Aesthetic:** High-density, technical, and high-contrast.

### Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Inter`
- **Characteristics:** Exceptional legibility at small sizes, neutral, and precise. It's the industry standard for high-performance SaaS tools.

### Color Palette
- **Primary Accent:** Gold/Brass (#D4AF37).
- **Backgrounds:** Deep Obsidian (#0E0E0E) and Gunmetal.
- **Surface:** Tonal shifts rather than shadows to define hierarchy.

### UI Components
- **Roundness:** `ROUND_FOUR` (Sharp, precise corners).
- **Elevation:** Flat, using borders and background color shifts to separate layers.
